Given `WxT` makes use of the [Lightning][lightning] base framework
providing extensive integration with the [Panels][panels] ecosystem of tools:

- [CTools][ctools]
- [Page Manager][page_manager]
- [Panels][panels]
- [Panelizer][panelizer]
